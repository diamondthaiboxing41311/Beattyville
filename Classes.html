We currently offer the following membership categories:

  • Group Membership - train with an instructor and a group of students. Group memberships include weekly classes and open gym time.

  • Private Membership - train one on one with an instructor without the distraction of other students. Private memberships include sessions, classes, and open gym time.

  • Youth Membership - learn self defense, build selfesteem, and learn to work as team in our youth training program. Our staff is available to answer any questions you may have.

    • There are three categories of gym time:

  • Session - Learn one on one with an instructor

  • Class - Learn with an instructor and a group of students

  • Open Gym - Train using gym equipment either alone or with gym members
