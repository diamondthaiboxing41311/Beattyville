What is Thai Boxing?

Thai Boxing, sometimes referred to as "Muay Thai", is a combat sport that uses stand-up striking along with various clinching techniques. This discipline is known as the "art of eight limbs" as it is characterized by the combined use of fists, elbows, knees and shins. Thai Boxing is more than just a practical system for self-defense. It is a fun and effective way to get in shape and promote personal health and well being.
